African Violet

Plant Care Information

  • LIGHT: Never place your african violet in direct sunlight. For flowers to bloom, brighter light is needed.
  • HUMIDITY: This plant requires high humidity, but avoid misting the leaves directly.
  • WATERING: Keep compost moist and allow it to dry out slightly between watering's.
  • Average warmth at 15°C - 21°C / 60°F - 72°F. Try to keep the temperature above 15°C / 60°F in Winter.
